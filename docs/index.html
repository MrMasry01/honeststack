What HonestStack is

HonestStack is a content engine built around one show: a daily World Cup 2026 roundup, hosted by a 2D animated Egyptian Pharaoh and narrated in colloquial Egyptian Arabic. Five to six football stories per video — the upsets, the squad shocks, the records, the goals — delivered fast, with the wit of a friend reacting to the news with you, not a reporter reading it at you.

Behind the scenes, it watches the football web continuously, clusters the day's stories, scores them by virality and drama, writes the script in a real Egyptian voice, illustrates each beat with a verified player photo or original brand-styled artwork, and ships the finished short-form video to your feed.

How it works

01 · SCRAPE

The football web

Every six hours, HonestStack pulls fresh stories from public sources — major outlets and tier-one journalists.

02 · SCRIPT

The voice

The day's stories are clustered, scored, and written into a tight 5-to-6 story roundup in colloquial Egyptian Arabic — one breath of news, jokes, and reactions.

03 · RENDER

The Pharaoh

The mascot narrates. Each story gets a verified real photo or an original brand-styled illustration. A finished short-form MP4 in minutes.

04 · PUBLISH

Your feed

Out to TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. Four videos a day, every day of the tournament.

The voice
