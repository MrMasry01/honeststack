HonestStack — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 May 2026

1. About HonestStack

HonestStack is an automated content engine that produces short-form football news videos about the FIFA World Cup and publishes them to social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube) on behalf of its operator. It is a single-operator publishing tool, not a consumer service.

2. Information we handle

  • Platform credentials. When the operator connects a TikTok, Instagram/Meta, or YouTube/Google account, HonestStack stores the resulting access and refresh tokens securely. They are used only to publish video content to those connected accounts and to retrieve basic, aggregate performance metrics (view, like, and comment counts) for the operator's own posts.
  • Public football news. HonestStack aggregates publicly available football news from public RSS feeds and public social media posts to generate video scripts.

We do not collect personal information from viewers of the published videos. We do not use cookies or advertising trackers.

3. How we use information

Solely to (a) generate and publish video content to the operator's authorized accounts, and (b) report aggregate post performance to the operator. We do not sell, rent, or share data with third parties for advertising.

4. Third-party services

HonestStack relies on the TikTok Content Posting API, the Meta/Instagram Graph API, the YouTube Data API, Supabase (database and storage), and the Anthropic API (script generation). Each processes data only as needed to operate the service and under its own terms.

5. Data storage and security

Data is stored in Supabase with row-level security. Access tokens are stored as encrypted secrets and are never exposed publicly.

6. Data retention and deletion

The operator may revoke any connected platform's access at any time from that platform's own settings, which immediately ends HonestStack's access. To request deletion of stored data, contact us at the address below.

7. Children

HonestStack is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.
